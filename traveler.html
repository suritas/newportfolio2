Family Traveler Smart TV & Mobile App
Family Traveler Smart TV & Mobile App

The following product was ideated as a group capstone project with 3 other Master's Candidates at the University of Michigan. The prototype and maps below were created by myself.

1
PERSONA

Following our preliminary research, I created a persona that reflected our target user's most prominent concerns and goals when it camme to planning family trips. We then translated these into interactions offered by the product.

2
USER STORY MAP

Potential interactions were narrowed down based on their popularity amongst interviewees. These were then organized into a User Story Map. This exhibited the first outline of what a user's journey through the app (From Mobile to Smart TV) might look like.

3
VOICE UI MAP

Map of voice interactions for the Smart TV app.

4
PROTOTYPE

The prototype was created by me, using Adobe XD. Grapics and Icons were created from scratch with Microsoft Powerpoint & Adobe illustrator, and from Shuterstock Vector Icons stock.
